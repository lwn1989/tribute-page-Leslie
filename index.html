Cheung Kwok Wing


One of the founding fathers of Cantopop

The photo of cheung kwok wing

Here's a time line of Cheung Kwok Wing's life:

  • 1956 - Born in Hongkong
  • 1968 - He was sent to Norwich School in England
  • 1988 - Received Most Popular Male Artist Awards
  • 1989 - He received Jade Solid Gold Best Ten Music Awards
  • 1989 - He announced his retirement from the music industry as a pop singer
  • 1999 - he won the Golden Needle Award for his outstanding achievement as a musician at the RTHK Top 10 Gold Songs Awards
  • 2000 - He was honoured as "Asia's Biggest Superstar" at the 2000 CCTV-MTV Music Honours
  • 2003 - dies in Hongkong
